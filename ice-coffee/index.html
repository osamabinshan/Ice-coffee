Ice Coffee

Discover how to make iced coffee at home. With simple recipe, you can get a
cooling caffeine fix with no fuss. Try it with a shot of maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 200ml strong black coffee
  • 50ml milk
  • ice
  • maple syrup, optional

Method

  1. Make a 200ml cup of black coffee, then allow the coffee to go completely cold.
  2. Pour into a blender with the milk along with 2 or 3 handfuls of ice and maple syrup, if using, then blend until smooth and foamy.
  3. Pour into a chilled tall glass and serve.
